The original project used a toy Dyson map ($\lambda' = z\lambda^2/(1+\lambda)^2$) for hierarchical RG that produced negative critical exponents ($\nu < 0$) for $p \ge 5$. The Lerner-Missarov papers establish the fundamental result: **p-adic QFT on $\mathbb{Q}_p$ discretized on $\mathcal{D}_p$ IS Dyson's hierarchical model** (Theorem 2.1 of TMF 4734). This provides a first-principles derivation of the hierarchical RG recursion from the Wilson RG applied to the p-adic field theory.

## 2. Mathematical Framework

### 2.1 Fundamental Connection

From Lerner & Missarov (1989, Commun. Math. Phys. 121, p. 35):

> "The scalar models of the field theory over the p-adic field $\mathbb{Q}_p$ are the natural continuous analogs of Dyson's hierarchical models."

> "The discretization of the field theory over $\mathbb{Q}_p$ on the hierarchical lattice of p-adic numbers with zero integer part is a model of Dyson's type."

### 2.2 Gaussian Hamiltonian

The discretization of the gaussian scaling-invariant random field on $\mathbb{Q}_p$ (Eq. 2.12-2.13) gives:

$$H_0 = -\frac{1}{2} \sum_{i,j} d_{ij} \xi_i \xi_j$$

where the covariance matrix elements $d_{ij}$ are determined by the p-adic distance structure on $\mathcal{D}_p$.

### 2.3 Wilson RG Transformation

The RG transformation (Eq. 2.17, Eq. 3.3):

$$R_a H = H_0 + \ln\langle\exp[(R_a^{\text{sh}\lambda} H')(\sigma_0 + \sigma_1)]\rangle_{\mu(d\sigma_1)}$$

where the average is with respect to the gaussian measure with correlation:

$$\langle\sigma_1(k_1)\sigma_1(k_2)\rangle = \delta(k_1 + k_2) \, ||k_1||^{1-a} (\chi_{\lambda\Omega}(k_1) - \chi_\Omega(k_1))$$

### 2.4 Bifurcation and Beta Function

**Bifurcation point:** $a_0 = 3/2$ — at this value, the $\phi^4$ interaction becomes marginal (eigenvalue = 1 under scaling).

**Beta function** (Eq. 2.25):

$$\beta(u) = \varepsilon u + \sum_{n \ge 2} c_n u^n, \quad \varepsilon = a - 3/2$$

**Non-gaussian fixed point** (Eq. 2.27):

$$\varepsilon + \sum_{n \ge 2} c_n u^{n-1}_* = 0 \quad \Rightarrow \quad u_* = -\varepsilon / c_2 + O(\varepsilon^2)$$

### 2.5 Hierarchical RG Recursion

The one-loop recursion for the coupling $\lambda$ at block size $p$:

$$\lambda' = p^{2a-3} \cdot (\lambda - c_2 \lambda^2)$$

where $c_2 \approx 3$ for the standard $\phi^4$ normalization.

**Key difference from toy Dyson map:**

| | Toy Dyson Map | Proper (Lerner-Missarov) |
|:--|:-------------|:-------------------------|
| Recursion | $\lambda' = (p+1)\lambda^2/(1+\lambda)^2$ | $\lambda' = p^{2a-3}(\lambda - c_2\lambda^2)$ |
| Scaling factor | $z = p+1$ (phenomenological) | $p^{2a-3}$ (derived from p-adic scaling) |
| Bifurcation | None (no parameter) | $a_0 = 3/2$ (marginality threshold) |
| Connection to p-adic QFT | None | Direct (Theorem 2.1) |
