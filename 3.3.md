## 1. Executive Summary

Module D5 computes the physical energy scale where the adelic structure becomes essential — specifically, where all completions of the Veneziano amplitude converge (the symmetric point $a = 0.5$ where $\Gamma_v = 1$ for all $v$).

**Result:** The symmetric point corresponds to an energy scale $\mu \sim 10^{85}$ GeV — near the Landau pole of QED. This means the adelic structure is invisible at collider-accessible energies ($m_e$ to $M_Z$) and only becomes essential at the scale where QED perturbation theory breaks down.

## 2. Key Results

### 2.1 Symmetric Point Scale

Using the ansatz $a(\mu) \approx \alpha(\mu)$ (neglecting the $\alpha' s$ term in the Regge trajectory):

$$\alpha(\mu) = \frac{\alpha_0}{1 - \beta_0 \alpha_0 \ln(\mu/m_e)}, \quad \beta_0 = \frac{2}{\pi}$$

Solving $\alpha(\mu_{\text{sym}}) = 0.5$:

$$\mu_{\text{sym}} = m_e \exp\left[\frac{1 - \alpha_0/0.5}{\beta_0 \alpha_0}\right] \approx 10^{85} \text{ GeV}$$

This is $\sim 10^5$ times below the Landau pole ($\mu_L \approx 10^{90}$ GeV).

**Physical interpretation:** At the symmetric point, all Archimedean and $p$-adic completions coincide. This is the "unification scale" in the adelic sense — not a GUT scale but the energy where the Veneziano amplitude's structure is fully adelically symmetric.

### 2.2 Scale Where Veneziano Beta Matches QED Beta

The Veneziano amplitude beta matches the QED one-loop coefficient at:

$$\beta_\infty(a) = \beta_0 = \frac{2}{\pi} \quad \Rightarrow \quad a \approx 0.4, \quad \mu \sim 3 \times 10^{91} \text{ MeV} \sim 10^{82} \text{ GeV}$$

This is deeper into the UV than the symmetric point, beyond the Landau pole. It confirms that $\beta_\infty$ and $\beta_{\text{QED}}$ operate at fundamentally different scales.

### 3.2 Three Regimes of the Adelic Theory

1. **Perturbative Regime ($\mu < 10^{85}$ GeV):** The adelic constraint is satisfied but invisible. $\beta_\infty \neq 0$ describes the observed running; $\sum\beta_p = -\beta_\infty$ cancels it exactly in the adelic sum. This is consistent with all collider data.

2. **Adelic Regime ($\mu \sim 10^{85}$ GeV, $a \sim 0.5$):** All completions coincide — the Veneziano amplitude becomes fully symmetric. This is where the Archimedean and $p$-adic physics are truly unified. The compactification ratio $R = 8.44$ is determined here.

3. **Non-perturbative Regime ($\mu > 10^{85}$ GeV):** QED perturbation theory fails. The adelic structure must be treated non-perturbatively. The Landau pole is eliminated not by making $\alpha$ constant (M11's error) but by the full adelic beta function's structure.

### 3.3 Testability

**Direct testability:** The adelic unification scale ($\sim 10^{85}$ GeV) is far beyond any conceivable collider. No direct experimental test is possible.

**Indirect testability:** The compactification ratio $R = \beta_\infty(0.5)/\beta_0 = 8.44$ constrains Calabi-Yau compactifications. If a specific CY manifold's intersection numbers produce this ratio, it would provide indirect evidence for the adelic structure.
